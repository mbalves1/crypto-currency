Dacxi Coin.
The cryptocurrency of the Dacxi Chain.

Introducing Dacxi Coin. A hero of the new Crypto 4.0 revolution, where blockchain technology has the power to solve major issues that affect billions of people all over the world.

You can read more about Dacxi Coin and the Dacxi Chain here.

Dacxi Coin's use cases

International investment transfers. As the Dacxi Chain's native currency, Dacxi Coin will be used to enable global crowdfunding investments.

Blockchain fees. Fees on the Dacxi Chain will only be able to be paid in Dacxi Coin.

Node staking. Nodes in the Dacxi blockchain will validate transactions before they are added to the blockchain and for this, they will earn fees. To own a node, Dacxi licensees must 'stake' Dacxi Coins thus taking them out of circulation. This 'Proof of Stake' protocol is commonplace in the current crypto ecosystem.

Exchange coin. On token exchanges, the Dacxi Coin will provide global liquidity for investors.

E-commerce. Within the investor community, Dacxi Coin will be used to make global product payments.
